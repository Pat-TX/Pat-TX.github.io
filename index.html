About Me!

Hey! My name is Patrick Williams. I'm a student at the University of Texas at Arlington pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Truthfully, I was an electrical engineering major for the longest time, but then I took my first programming class, and, well... Here we are! I fell in love with software engineering, especially the algorithm analysis side of things. I love learning all there is to learn through my classes and my own self study, which, up to this point, has primarily been front-end web development -- which brought me to develop my own website. I have aspirations to become a full stack software engineer, but I also have a side-eye on AI and machine learning.

I grew up in a small town in Kentucky, and that was my stomping grounds until I was 18. I graduated from Meade County High School and began my academic career at Purdue University in West Lafayette in 2019. The COVID-19 pandemic hit, and that's where I decided to take a year break from school to see if that situation would die down -- but that didn't stop me from learning on my own! Eventually, I moved to Texas, joined a company as a Geographic Information Systems intern in mid 2021, and started my education back up again at Tarrant County College. 2 years later, I'm still at that company as a Business and Technology Specialist, continuing my education at UTA, and incredibly excited about what's over the horizon.

If you want to work with me, head down to my contact page -- I would love to hear from you!
